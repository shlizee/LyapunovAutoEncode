 
 ''Six Stories'' is a short story collection by Stephen King, published in 1997 by Philtrum Press. It is Limited edition books|limited to 1100 copies, which are signed and numbered. ''Six Stories'' contains: 
 
 *  " Lunch at the Gotham Cafe "  (later published as part of ''Everything's Eventual: 14 Dark Tales|Everything's Eventual'', slightly revised) 
 *  " L. T.'s Theory of Pets "  (later published as part of ''Everything's Eventual: 14 Dark Tales|Everything's Eventual'') 
 *  " Luckey Quarter "  (later published as part of ''Everything's Eventual: 14 Dark Tales|Everything's Eventual'') 
 *  " Autopsy Room Four "  (later published as part of ''Everything's Eventual: 14 Dark Tales|Everything's Eventual'') 
 *  " Blind Willie "  (later published as part of ''Hearts in Atlantis'', heavily revised) 
 *  " The Man in the Black Suit "  (later published as part of ''Everything's Eventual: 14 Dark Tales|Everything's Eventual'', slightly revised)
